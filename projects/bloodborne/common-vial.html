Common Blood Vial Replica

Perhaps the most iconic item out of From Software's Bloodborne is the Blood Vial. And because Bloodborne is one of my favorite games, I had to replicate the vial for my collection.

To help others who want to do similar builds, all of the insight I have on this project is here in this webpage, along with all of the IP I created. After all, the purpose of this site is to share information for free to everyone.
